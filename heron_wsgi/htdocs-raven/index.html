Raven Research Portal: Epilogue

Materials that were available via the RAVEN portal are being integrated into the overall web site of Frontiers: The Heartland Institute for Clinical and Translational Research.

Meanwhile, qualified KUMC faculty and sponsored users are welcome to visit the HERON Research Data Repository for hypothesis generation and cohort discovery with i2b2.

We recommend that first-time HERON users read Getting Started with HERON or join us at the Frontiers Clinical/Translational Informatics Clinic, 4pm every other Tuesday in Library Room 1040. (See the KUMC Calendar for the date of the next clinic.)

For light-weight research data capture, we also offer REDCap to the Frontiers research community. Access is administered by KUMC staff, so if you are a researcher in the Frontiers community, but you do not have a KUMC account of some sort, please submit a Frontiers Affiliate Account Request.
